Amorphous solid

Download AViz files

In condensed matter physics and materials science, an amorphous or non-crystalline solid is a solid that lacks the long-range order characteristic of a crystalยน.

This example contains a large amorphous solid, so below there are "close-ups" of some parts of the structure:

Close-up #1: Around (10, 3, 8), cube of size 3

Command used to generate this close-up:

extract_box.py amorphous.xyz -x 10 -y 3 -z 8 -s 3

Close-up #2: Around (10, 10, 10), cube of size 4

Command used to generate this close-up:

extract_box.py amorphous.xyz -x 10 -y 10 -z 10 -s 4

Close-up #3: Around (9, 8, 6), cube of size 2

Command used to generate this close-up:

extract_box.py amorphous.xyz -x 9 -y 8 -z 6 -s 2
